# 3D Vehicle Models

This directory contains 3D vehicle models in GLTF/GLB format.

## Free Car Models to Download

Download these free car models and place them in this directory:

### Recommended Free Sources:

1. **Sketchfab** (Free models with Creative Commons license)
   - https://sketchfab.com/search?q=car&type=models&licenses=322a749bcfa841b29dff1e8a1bb74b0b
   - Filter by: Downloadable, Free
   - Download format: GLTF (.glb)

2. **Free3D**
   - https://free3d.com/3d-models/car
   - Look for models with .glb or .gltf format

3. **Quaternius** (100% free, no attribution required)
   - https://quaternius.com/packs/ultimatevehicles.html
   - Download the pack, extract GLB files

## Suggested Models:

### Sedan
- Search: "sedan car low poly"
- File: `sedan.glb`

### SUV
- Search: "suv car low poly"
- File: `suv.glb`

### Truck
- Search: "pickup truck low poly"
- File: `truck.glb`

### Coupe
- Search: "sports car low poly"
- File: `coupe.glb`

## Instructions:

1. Download the GLB files from the links above
2. Rename them to: `sedan.glb`, `suv.glb`, `truck.glb`, `coupe.glb`
3. Place them in this directory: `public/models/`
4. The app will automatically load them

## Model Requirements:

- Format: `.glb` (preferred) or `.gltf`
- Size: Under 5MB each
- Polygons: Under 50k triangles (low-poly for performance)
- License: Free to use commercially

## License Info:

Make sure to check the license of each model. Look for:
- Creative Commons Zero (CC0) - No attribution required
- Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY) - Attribution required
- Free for commercial use
